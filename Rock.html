ROCK

About this Genre

Rock dates back to the 1940's and 50's with artists such as Elvis combining
blues music with a "rock and roll" style tempo. Rock continued to grow in
popularity, reinventing music with bands like the Beatles and the Beach Boys, and
even inventing new genres of music, like metal, punk and grunge. This genre keeps
creating new and exciting sounds every day.

Rock's versatility can attract many listeners, with some artists being heavy
and fast, and some being slower and moore soothing. Rock music can create
music enjoyable for anyone.
